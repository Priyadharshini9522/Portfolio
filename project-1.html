Business Intelligence Solution for AtliQ Grands

Description

  • In this project, I developed a Business Intelligence solution using Power BI to help AtliQ Grands, a luxury hotel chain, regain its market share and improve revenue.
  • The solution provides key insights into occupancy rates, revenue trends, pricing strategies, and customer preferences based on historical data.
  • The dashboard enables the Revenue Management Team to make data-driven decisions by identifying areas of revenue leakage, optimizing pricing strategies, and understanding customer booking behavior.

    • Key Learnings & Skills Applied:
    • Data Cleaning & Transformation using Power Query for seamless data processing.
    • Data Modeling & Relationships to integrate multiple datasets effectively.
    • DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for advanced calculations and KPI analysis.
    • Dynamic Visualizations & Dashboards for clear, actionable insights.
    • Trend Analysis & Forecasting to optimize pricing and occupancy rates.
    • Storytelling with Data to assist stakeholders in strategic decision-making.

    This project highlights how Business Intelligence can transform decision-making, helping AtliQ Grands recover lost revenue, enhance operational efficiency, and stay competitive in the luxury hospitality industry.
